What is a UTR/Transaction ID?

UTR (Unique Transaction Reference) is a code generated after you make a UPI or bank transfer. It helps us verify your payment and activate your order.

You can find the UTR in your payment app (like PhonePe, GPay, Paytm, etc.) after you complete the payment. It usually looks something like:
1234567890ABC

Just copy that UTR and paste it in the UTR/Transaction ID field on the Order Page.
